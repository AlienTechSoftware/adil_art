About Me

Dr. Adil Adnan,

A respected member of the German GA Society of Physicians and the American Society of Medical Mycologists, holds diverse qualifications including dermatology certification and alternative medicine training. With extensive experience as a former university professor and an innovative researcher, Dr. Adnan has developed groundbreaking hypotheses and medications for treating a wide range of diseases.

  • Member of the German GA Society of Physicians / Germany.
  • Member of the American Society of Medical Mycologists / United States of America.
  • Dermatology Certificate, School of Medicine, Novosibirsk University / United States of America.
  • Alternative Medicine Certificate, School of Medicine, University of Minnesota / United States of America.
  • Master's in Plant Diseases / Mycology, College of Agricultural Engineering Sciences, University of Baghdad, Iraq.
  • Master's in Science, University of Austin / United States of America.
  • Former university professor in genetic engineering for graduate studies / University of Baghdad - Iraq.
  • Proprietor of the Bio-Spy hypothesis and Cell Starveness hypothesis for disease treatment.
  • Inventor of numerous medications that treat dozens of common and stubborn diseases.
